Process

My priority is to launch on time and keep you informed through every step of the project.
To easily identify your project status, I've identified the six milestones.

  • Discovery

    We begin the process with a client application.
    Once the application is submitted, a link to schedule a discovery call will become available.

  • Research

    Conduct research into your industry and develop a plan to implement into your website.

  • Development

    Custom coding and design of you website begins.

  • Refine

    The website is made available to you for review. 3 rounds of adjustments are provided to lock in any changes.

  • Launch

    The exciting part!
    We lauch your site for the world to see.

  • Support

    Technical support is provided for certain issues for a period of time after launch.
